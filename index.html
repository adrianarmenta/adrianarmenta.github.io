ESports And Why It's A Growing Industry

Over the years gaming was considered just a hobby for many but as the industry has grown and developed it's become so much more than that. Competitive gaming has become mainstream and is still gaining success with the TSM being valued at $410 Million. Gaming has become a billion dollar industry where people can actually make a living off of gaming. Not only is their opportunity in ESports, but with the growth of Twitch and Youtube it's now possible to make a career off of live streaming video games.
